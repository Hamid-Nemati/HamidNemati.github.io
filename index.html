About Me

Hello! I'm Hamid.
I am a master’s student at the University of Tehran, and I have the pleasure of working with Hesham Faili, Masoud Asadpour, and Ahmad Kalhor on innovative applications of artificial intelligence (AI) in the realm of finance and economics. I am an active member in both natural language processing (NLP) and social network labs, where I engage in cutting-edge research.
